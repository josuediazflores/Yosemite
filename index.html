How readings work

Every reading states its age.

LIVE Live

Observed within the hour. Live readings pulse — only fresh data animates on the console.

RECENT Recent

Observed within fourteen days. Renders solid and still; current enough to plan around.

ARCHIVE Archive

Older, or undated by its source. Hollow and faded — context, not conditions.

WHEN A SOURCE FAILS · we say so, plainly: "Gage didn't answer. Next sweep in 15 min."

Ouzel YOSEMITE FIELD STATION

Named after the water ouzel, a small gray bird that hunts underwater in the Merced. It lives in the river all year, and it never stops reporting. Neither do we.
